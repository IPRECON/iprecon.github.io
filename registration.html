Registration Guidelines




  • Each paper must be covered by a full IEEE member or Non-Member registration fee by one of the authors.

  • IEEE members are requested to provide the IEEE membership number during the online registration process if registering under IEEE category. Authors claiming subsidized registration fees as IEEE members need to mail their IEEE membership card, active at the time of conference registration to iprecon2020@gmail.com, failing which they will be required to pay the applicable balance registration fee of the relevant category.

  • Students need to mail their valid student ID card to iprecon2020@gmail, if registering under student category failing which they will be required to pay the applicable balance registration fee of the relevant category.

  • Please keep a copy of the transaction ID, generated by the payment gateway as well as by your bank. You may need these later if you need to track your payment/registration.

  • All correspondence regarding your registration for IPRECON2020 should contain your Paper ID, and registration confirmation number

Cancellation and No Show Policy:




  • In case an author wants to cancel the registration, refund of registration fee will be made after deduction of cancellation charge (50% of the registration fees), if written notification is received before 1-October-2020. No refunds will be made after this date. Cancellation must be notified to the organizers in writing by sending e-mail to iprecon2020@gmail.com.

  • All bank service charges and all administration fees will be deducted from the refund. Note that for administrative reasons, refund will be done only after the conference.

  • Each accepted paper included in the final program is expected to have at least one author attend and present the paper at the conference. Non-presented paper will not be submitted to IEEE XploreÂ® digital library as per IEEE no-show policy.

Registration Steps



There are three steps in the registration process.

    Step 1

  • Click on the 'Book Now' option and Choose your registration as per category. Buy one registration for registering one paper. Buy additional registrations for registering additional papers. Also can increase the registration count by clicking the '+' button and click 'Proceed'. Over length manuscripts in excess of six pages is allowed after paying Rs. 500 for each additional page, for foreign authors, it is $10/additional page and corresponding tickets must be bought by Author

    • Step 2

  • Enter buyer details (Buyer name, Email, Phone number, etc). Anyone can buy the registration ticket. Please note that the invoice will be given in the name of the buyer.

    • Step 3

  • Enter attendee details. The first attendee is considered as the registering author. Please take extreme care to provide Correct paper ID. Click 'Continue' and check all the information provided in the payment page, Click 'Proceed to Pay'
